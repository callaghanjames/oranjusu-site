Freshly Squeezed: Future Funk is a selection of some of the world's finest funky electronic grooves. Designed to keep you motivated, moving and thriving throughout your day.

Brought to you by producer Oran Jusu, the playlist is constantly evolving with new hot picks every month. You'll find everything from Future Funk pioneers like Yung Bae, Macross 82.99 & Vantage to new wave and rising artists like Kouek, Corrout, _blip & Jansport George. Sit back and enjoy the vibes.
